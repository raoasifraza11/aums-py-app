Teaching and Learning Methodolog

Instruction and learning environment in the Department of Civil Engineering at Abasyn University comprises of:

  1. Classroom lectures, duly supported by audio-visual aids, demonstrations and relevant handouts.
  2. Assignments and tutorials requiring use of reference materials and internet facility. Term projects and classroom presentations.
  3. Laboratory experiments, field work, and design exercises
  4. Instructional visits to appropriate establishments, installations, construction sites, field stations, industries, etc.
  5. Lectures and seminars by renowned professionals from the industry. Enhanced use of modern computing facilities in the institution.
  6. The preparation of visit reports/notebooks/ field books/graphs and drawing sheets pertaining to the field work and practical work.
