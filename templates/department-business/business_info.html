Bachelor in Business Administration

The four years BBA program is tailored made to serve the needs of the bright young persons who have completed twelve years of education and are looking for a career education in entrepreneurship, management profession or towards higher education in business administration. This program is open to the students with diverse educational backgrounds including, humanities, science, arts and commerce. However, being a program with challenging curricula and content, it is accessible mainly to those students who have excellent academic record and high potential for success.
