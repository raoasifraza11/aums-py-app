Program Structure

Learning Outcomes

The students who earn the BBA degree will be able to:

  1. Communicate effectively and professionally and demonstrate the ability to create coherent written and oral statements with the diverse audiance across the cultures replicating skills to analyze and synthesize information.
  2. Demonstrate the ability to identify and evaluate relevant information for decision-making and make usage of diagnostic thinking skills and analytical techniques to access the information and solve problems in the environment like ours characterized by uncertainity.
  3. Understand the importance of teamwork and group dynamics in achieving organizational goals and demonstrate ability to work effectively in teams.

Area of Specialization

  1. Finance
  2. Human Resource Management
  3. Marketing
  4. Information Technology
  5. Management
  6. Project Management
  7. Supply Chain Management
