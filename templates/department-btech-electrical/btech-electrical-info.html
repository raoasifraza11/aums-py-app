Bachelor of Technology (Electrical)

The B. Tech Electrical Engineering program has been carefully designed to enable graduates to undertake operation and maintenance of electrical appliances by applying his/her knowledge in all stages of Electrical Engineering projects. The students are extensively exposed to the real electrical engineering projects that equip them to work in an industrial environment. B. Tech Electrical Engineering is a 4 years (132 credit hours) program with an average work load of 12-15 credit hours per Trimester.
