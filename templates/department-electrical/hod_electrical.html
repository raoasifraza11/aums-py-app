Department of Electrical Engineering


A very warm welcome to our departmental webpage. The mission of the Electrical Engineering program is to provide quality education, strive to impart critical thinking and creativity using latest technologies adhering to a sense of social responsibility and teamwork skills. Our Department offers a wide range of courses at bachelors and master level with specialization in power as well as communication domain. The Department has implemented Outcome Based Education (OBE) system starting spring 2018. We have experienced faculty members to teach courses and supervise state of the art research. The department has well-equipped laboratories along with dedicated lab engineers. Other than performing the teaching responsibilities, the faculty is also involved in extra- curricular activities to provide a conducive environment for students. We welcome you to browse the department website, whether you are a student, parent, alum, we are looking forward for you to visit us. Your feedback is highly appreciated.

Department Mission

The mission of the Electrical Engineering department is to “excel in quality education and research adhering to a sense of social responsibility and teamwork skills”.
