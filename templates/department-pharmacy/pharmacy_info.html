Doctor of Pharmacy

Pharmacy is a profession which enables its practitioners to be involved in a multifaceted service of humanity; starting right from innovation of a new drug candidate to the patient care. Pharmacy is a profession concerned with the art and science of preparing medicine to be used in prevention, diagnosis and treatment of the diseases. A pharmacist has to have the knowledge of identification, selection, isolation, analysis, standardization, pharmacological and therapeutic actions and safe uses of drugs and medicines. Any healthcare team is incomplete without their services.
